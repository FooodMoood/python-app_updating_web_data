<p class="has-large-font-size"><strong>Dietary Curiosity</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<p class="has-medium-font-size">
Chili peppers are not only a popular spice for adding heat to dishes, but they also come with impressive health benefits. One of the key compounds in chili, capsaicin, has been shown to boost metabolism and aid in weight loss by increasing the body's heat production and energy expenditure. Additionally, capsaicin has anti-inflammatory properties and can help reduce pain by desensitizing pain receptors over time. Eating chili peppers may also promote heart health by improving blood flow and reducing cholesterol levels. So, adding a bit of spice to your meals can contribute to both flavor and health.</p>

<p class="has-large-font-size"><strong>Recipe</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol><!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Cut the chicken into smaller pieces. Add oregano, sweet pepper, cinnamon, chili, salt, pepper, lemon juice and garlic. Set aside the marinated chicken for 10 minutes.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Heat a pan with olive oil. Add the marinated chicken to the heated olive oil and fry until golden brown.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Cut the lettuce, red onion and tomatoes into small pieces.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Cut the garlic into small pieces.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Mix garlic, marjoram, natural yogurt and mayonnaise together to obtain garlic sauce.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Prepare 3 tortillas.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Place chopped lettuce in the center of each pancake.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Add tomatoes and baked chicken to the lettuce.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Sprinkle the prepared ingredients over the onion and add the garlic sauce on top.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
<!-- wp:list-item {"fontSize":"medium"} -->
<li class="has-medium-font-size">Roll everything into a cake and fry it in a pan so that the tortilla does not fall apart.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->
</ol>
